## 1. Who We Are

We develop and publish the mobile chess game _Chess: Be a Grandmaster_. This policy explains what data we handle — which is essentially none.

---

## 2. No Registration, No Accounts

The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information whatsoever to use any feature, including multiplayer.

---

## 3. No Data Collection or Storage

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. Specifically:

- We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other identifier.
- We do not store game history, match results, or any gameplay data — on your device or on any server.
- We have no database. There is no backend storage of any kind.
- Multiplayer functionality is handled entirely in real-time with no records retained once a session ends.
- We do not use cookies or any equivalent tracking technology.
- We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data.
- We do not use analytics SDKs or crash-reporting tools that transmit personal data.

## 4. In-App Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. **We do not control what data these ad networks collect.** Ad networks may independently collect certain information in accordance with their own privacy policies, such as:

- Device advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
- General device information (device type, OS version)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

You may limit ad tracking through your device settings:

- **iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
- **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or opt out of personalized ads

Multiplayer sessions are conducted in real-time. No player information, moves, chat, or results are stored at any point. When a session ends, no record of it exists anywhere.

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, please contact us — though there is no personal data for us to access or delete.
